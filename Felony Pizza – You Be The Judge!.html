Do you offer gluten-free menu options?

Yes. Our menu has several items that are gluten-free and are marked by a special symbol. We offer a 12 inch Gluten free crust, as well as Daiya Vegan cheese. 

View our Menu page for a full list of menu items, including our gluten-free dishes.

I have an allergy. How should I inform staff?

Please inform your server when ordering, and they will be sure to let the kitchen know so that appropriate steps can be taken.

Do you offer catering?

At this time, we do offering catering on a case by case basis. You can contact us about catering at felonypizzaoffices@gmail.com
